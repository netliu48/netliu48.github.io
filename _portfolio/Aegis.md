## Overview
Aegis is an infinite FPS arena built on precision, momentum, and controlled aggression. Your shield isn’t just defense, it’s your weapon. Charge it, ricochet it off walls, and tear through enemies as each round grows faster, deadlier, and more relentless.
The overall idea of the gameplay is that you as the player are working to progressively get stronger using the power of one of the four gods; Zeus, Poseidon, Gaia, and Hades. Each area and upgrades for the player combat are based upon each of these gods and will be achieveable within their designated area. Visuals of the gameplay and the overall story of overcoming the monsters using power of the gods was to emulate the feeling of awe and wonder for the theme of the game jam.
