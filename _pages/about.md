Hi, my name is Annette and I primarily focus on the art side of video games. Whether it
is concept art, texturing, UI design, I like to try learning all the skills in order to make what the
eyes see in a game.

What motivates me as a game designer is finding the joy that comes from the creations I
make. Similarly to my love for food, I enjoy throwing some seasonings into the game to make it
feel alive, look aesthetically pleasing, and, most of all, add a little something to keep the players
coming back. Building that community and seeing the excitement in players is what brings me
joy and motivation to work on games.
